About Draford Eye Care Clinic

Draford Eye Care Clinic Limited is a professional eye care clinic established to provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality eye health services to individuals and families in Nigeria. Founded with a vision to make quality eye care accessible, we have been serving our community with dedication and excellence.

Our clinic is staffed by experienced optometrists and eye care professionals who are committed to preserving and enhancing your vision. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to ensure accurate assessments and provide personalized treatment plans for each patient.

At Draford Eye Care, we believe that good vision is essential for quality of life. Our patient-centered approach ensures that you receive the care and attention you deserve in a comfortable and welcoming environment.
